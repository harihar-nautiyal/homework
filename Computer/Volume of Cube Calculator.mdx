# Volume of a Cube Calculator

This program calculates the volume of a cube.

<CodeBlock
language="python"
filename="cubeVolumeCalculator.py"
code={`
def calculate_cube_volume(side):
return side ** 3

side = int(input("Enter the length of a side of the cube: "))
volume = calculate_cube_volume(side)
print(f"The volume of the cube is: {volume}")
`}
